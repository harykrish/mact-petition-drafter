## 6. HEADS OF COMPENSATION

**Computation of loss of future earning capacity** (per Sarla Verma adaptation × Pranay Sethi future prospects):

- Annual income: ₹72,00,000 [F032]
- Future prospects: **+25%** — self-employed [F030], age band **40–50** [F002] (Pranay Sethi, self-employed table)
- Multiplier: **13** — age **48**, band **46–50** [F002] (Sarla Verma table)
- Functional disability: **80%** [F022]

> Loss of future earning = 7,200,000 × (1 + 0.25) × 13 × 0.80
> = 7,200,000 × 1.25 × 13 × 0.80 = **₹9,36,00,000**

**Medical expenses (sum of itemised medical_expense_* facts):**
- Neurosurgery / cervical fixation: 1,500,000 [F024]
- Neuro-ICU / ventilator: 2,000,000 [F025]
- Imaging: 400,000 [F026]
- Medicines / consumables: 600,000 [F027]
- Neuro-rehabilitation: 500,000 [F028]
- **Total = ₹50,00,000**

**Loss of income during treatment (actual, 45 days hospitalisation [F018]):**
7,200,000 × 45 / 365 = **₹8,87,671**
